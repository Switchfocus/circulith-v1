Process

Four steps.
Closed loop.
Zero waste.

Every tyre that enters our facility is fully processed. Syngas produced during pyrolysis recirculates to fuel the reactor — no external energy input required.

01
Collection
Free, scheduled pickup from your site. All tyre types accepted — passenger, truck, agricultural, industrial. Full documentation provided.
Free pickup
02
Shredding
Tyres are mechanically shredded. Steel wire is extracted and separated at this stage for immediate recovery.
Steel extracted
03
Pyrolysis
Rubber granulate enters a sealed, oxygen-free reactor. Controlled heat decomposes it into oil vapour and carbon char. Syngas loops back to power the process.
Closed energy loop
04
Recovery & Sale
Carbon black, pyrolysis oil, and steel wire are tested, certified, and delivered to industrial buyers. Every output has a destination.

Why
Circulith.

We built a model where zero-fee recycling isn't a marketing promise — it's a structural consequence of how the economics work.

01
Zero fees, structurally
Our revenue comes entirely from recovered material sales. We don't charge partners a fee and call it sustainable — the cost is zero because the model works without it.
02
Local by design
Luxembourg-based, Greater Region coverage. Short logistics chains cut transport emissions. Accountability is direct — one team, one location, no intermediaries.
03
Thermally self-sufficient
Syngas produced during pyrolysis recirculates to fuel the reactor. No external fossil fuel consumed in processing. Every input is utilised; every output has a buyer.
04
Documented and traceable
Every collection batch, every certified output — documented under EU waste framework directives. Luxembourg environmental permits in place. Full traceability for your compliance records.
